Our Mission & Vision

YouthConnect is a vibrant community for young people seeking to grow in their faith while building meaningful friendships. We believe in creating a safe space where youth can explore spirituality, ask questions, and develop a personal relationship with God.

Our mission is to empower the next generation of Christian leaders through biblical teaching, authentic community, and service opportunities. We meet every Friday at 7 PM for worship, teaching, and fellowship.

"Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example for the believers in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity."
1 Timothy 4:12
